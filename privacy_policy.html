VIII. How to Opt Out

Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

For iOS Devices:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  2. Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  3. Or toggle off tracking for specific apps

To disable Apple's personalized ads:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  2. Toggle off "Personalized Ads"

For Android Devices:

  1. Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  2. Select "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  3. Or reset your advertising ID

Opt-Out of Location Tracking

For iOS Devices:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  2. Select SMART CLEANER
  3. Choose "Never" or "Ask Next Time"

For Android Devices:

  1. Go to Settings > Apps > SMART CLEANER
  2. Select Permissions > Location
  3. Choose "Don't allow"

Opt-Out of All Data Processing

If you no longer want us to process your personal information, contact us at karthikbakshi9@gmail.com. Note that opting out of all data processing may require you to uninstall the App, as certain functional data is necessary for the App to operate.
